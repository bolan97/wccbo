Bo Lan

Hello, I am a graduate student in Computer Arts. My undergraduate degree is in Architectural Interior Design. A lot of my work is filled with visual projects related to space and light, which involve a lot of architectural modeling and interior design space modeling fundamentals and computer visual art related techniques. In my work I want to complete my concept by using the medium of computer art and technical installation performance. My concept is an art installation inspired by the violation of children's rights. I want to call attention to children's living space and their rights in life.
